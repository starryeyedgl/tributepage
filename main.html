Andrew Lloyd Webber


Andrew Lloyd Webber, The composer of some of the world's best-known musicals


When Sunset Boulevard joined School Of Rock, Cats and The Phantom Of The Opera on Broadway in 2017, Andrew became the only person to equal the record set in 1953 by Rodgers and Hammerstein with four Broadway shows running concurrently. Other musicals he has composed include Aspects Of Love, Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat, Jesus Christ Superstar, Evita and Love Never Dies.


Follow Andrew's illutrious career in muscial theatre:

  • 1948 - Andrew Lloyd Webber was born in South Kensington to parents William and Jean Lloyd Webber.
  • 1951 - Andrew's brother and celebrated musician Julian was born.
  • 1965 - The 17-year-old budding musical-theatre composer was introduced to the 20-year-old aspiring pop-song writer Tim Rice. Their first collaboration was The Likes of Us.
  • 1967 - Novello requested a retelling of the biblical story of Joseph, in which Lloyd Webber and Rice humorously pastiched a number of pop-music styles.
  • 1968 - Joseph first premiered at Colet Court in a 15-minute pop cantata.
  • 1971 - Jesus Christ Superstar opened in New York at the Mark Hellinger Theatre.
  • 1972 - Jesus Christ Superstar opened in London at the Palace Theatre.
  • 1973 - Joseph premiered at the Albery Theatre in London.
  • 1977 - Andrew established the Really Useful Group.
  • 1978 - Evita opened in London at the Prince Edward Theatre.
  • 1981 - The premiere of Cats in London at the New London Theatre.
  • 1982 - Cats premiered at the Winter Garden Theatre in New York
  • 1986 - The Phantom of the Oprea opened in London at Her Majesty's Theatre.
  • 1992 - La Bete premiered in the West End.
  • 1994 - Sunset Boulevard opened on Broadway starring Glenn Close as Norma Desmond at the Minskoff Theatre.
  • 2002 - Bombay Dreams opens at the Apollo Victoria Theatre in London.
  • 2006 - Andrew in honored at the 29th prestigious Kennedy Center Honors in Washington,DC.
  • 2013 - Phantom celebrates its 25th anniversary on Broadway
  • 2015 - Andrew's production of School of Rock is the first British musical to have world premiered on Broadway.
  • 2017 - When Sunset Boulevard joined School of Rock, Cats and Phantom of the Oprea on Broadway, Andrew Lloyd Webber became the only person to equal the recard set in 1953 by Rodgers and Hammerstein with four Broadway shows running concurrently.
